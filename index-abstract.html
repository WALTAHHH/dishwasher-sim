Welcome to Dishwasher Simulator!

Let's learn the basics of keeping a kitchen running smoothly.

1. Grab Dirty Dishes

Press 1 to go to Intake, then Space to grab a dish.

💡 Dishes arrive in waves - don't let them pile up!

2. Load the Dishwasher

Press 2 for the dishwasher. Use WASD to move, Space to place.

Press R to rotate dishes for better fit!

💡 Plates, bowls, and cups go here. Pans and glasses need hand washing!

3. Run the Cycle

Press Enter to start washing. More dishes = better efficiency!

💡 You can't open it mid-cycle, so pack wisely!

4. Dry and Store

Move clean dishes to Drying (3) then Storage (4).

Stored dishes = points!

Ready to Roll!

Clear all dishes before time runs out. Good luck!
